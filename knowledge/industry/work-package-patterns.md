# Work-Package Patterns

A reusable work package states the outcome, authoritative inputs, boundaries,
acceptance checks, evidence expected at completion, and the next handoff.

For operational or technical work, keep these concerns separate:

- scope and assumptions;
- source-backed requirements;
- sequence and dependencies;
- safety or quality gates;
- deliverables and verification;
- unresolved questions.

This pattern is intentionally generic. It describes how to organize work, not
what any particular facility, customer, product, rate, or procedure requires.
